MLG@IITPKD

The Machine Learning Group (MLG@IITPKD) is a group of faculty members and students at the Indian Institute of Technology in Palakkad, who share interests in the broad areas of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science. The faculty members of the group are engaged in cutting-edge research on a rich variety of topics ranging from theoretical foundations to new algorithms and architectures as well as several exciting applications.
